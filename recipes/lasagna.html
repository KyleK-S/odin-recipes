Lasagna

Description

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store bought hamburger mixes! It should require about 10 minutes to prep and 35 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • spaghetti sauce
  • diced tomatoes
  • noodles
  • mozzarella
  • basil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the skillet over medium-high heat. Cook beef until browned.
  2. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot.
  3. Fill a large pot with water. Salt the water. Bring water to a boil. Cook noodles about 10 minutes. Drain the water.
  4. Add cooked noodles to the sauce and stir until coated.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella on top
  6. Preheat the oven's broiler and set the rack about 6 inches from the heat source.
  7. Place skillet in the broiler until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Wait for scalding hot lasagna to cool and then enjoy!
