Collard Greens

Description

If you don't like these, you just don't like collard greens, plain and simple. This recipe should take about 15m minutes to prep and around an hourto cook.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • country ham
  • chicken stock
  • soy sauce
  • collard greens
  • pepper vinegar
  • (optional) kimchi

Steps

  1. Cook onion and country ham in a large pot over medium-high heat until onions are soft.
  2. Add stock, soy sauce and the collard greens.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 30 minutes, covered.
  4. Season with salt and pepper with a couple dashes of pepper vinegar.
  5. (Optional) Add Kimchi and Enjoy!
