Beef Stew

Description

This simple and hearty stew is great for a cold winter day. It should take about 20 minutes to prep, and around an hour to cook.

Ingredients

  • beef stew meat
  • onion
  • beef broth
  • tomatoes
  • potatoes
  • celery
  • carrots
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add meat with onion and garlic. Cook just until meat is browned.
  2. Add broth, tomatoes, potatoes, celery, and carrots to the pot. Bring to a simmer and then reduce heat, cook uncovered until potatoes are soft and meat is tender.
  3. Serve with some warm bread and enjoy!
